Places lived
Identity as geography: the self as a travel log that keeps editing itself

Abhay was born in India and moved to the United States at age two. His early life included time in multiple countries, which he treats as “different operating systems” for the same person.

Paris

Paris appears in the narrative as an early imprint: museums, streets, and visual culture—an origin node for later painting.

Ireland

Ireland enters as a memory of weather, pace, and distance—useful for the “long-thinking” required by philosophy.

Stockholm (Sweden)

Stockholm, Sweden: a clean design language, a kind of calm systemization—echoes later in Abhay’s love for structured building via code.

Seattle

Seattle becomes the long-term anchor. It’s where Abhay identifies his ninth-grade pivot into coding + entrepreneurship, and where future ventures like A17 are imagined.
