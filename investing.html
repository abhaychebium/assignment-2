Investing
A private long-term game running in parallel to everything else

Abhay describes being “really interested in investing” and keeping his capital in investment accounts and stocks. In this hypertext artwork, investing functions like a time machine: it forces the present to negotiate with the future. (See: timeline.)

Why it matters in the identity map

Investing connects to entrepreneurship (ventures) through risk, to philosophy (philosophy) through uncertainty, and to health (health) through the idea of “long horizons.”
