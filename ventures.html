Entrepreneurship as a medium: prototypes of alternate selves

Abhay began pursuing entrepreneurship seriously after discovering coding in ninth grade (see education). His first major venture was Professor AI, built in 11th grade and sold in his senior year. After that, he paused—then returned with A17, described as a consumer app studio.

Professor AI

Professor AI is described as Abhay’s first “big venture”: a student-built startup that marked a transition from learning code to shipping products. In the hypertext logic of this project, Professor AI is a “first proof” that turns biography into a timeline (jump to timeline node).

A17 (consumer app studio)

A17 is an ongoing venture framed as a studio: a place where multiple products can be built, tested, and iterated quickly. In one future branch, A17 becomes the “operating system” for Abhay’s creativity—bridging art and engineering.

The “chill” period

After selling Professor AI, Abhay describes taking time to reset—less productivity, more reading and reflection. This period links outward into philosophy, painting, and investing.
