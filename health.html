Human health and anatomy
Curiosity sharpened by recovery

Abhay is deeply interested in human health and the mechanics of the body. He reports learning a lot about anatomy through recovering from broken bones: injury became an unexpected syllabus.

Anatomy as a hobby

Studying anatomy is treated here as practical philosophy: the body is where ideas meet limits. This page links to philosophy (questions), computer science (systems), and ventures (building health tools).

Alternative future: “health layer”

One future branch imagines Abhay turning this interest into a trusted “health layer”— a consumer product that makes knowledge about the body accessible. If you want that branch to feel more concrete, connect it to your studio work in A17.
