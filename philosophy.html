Philosophy and film study
Reading as a hobby; analysis as a habit

Abhay studies philosophy “on the side,” reading philosophical books and watching older films with an analytical lens. In this hypertext, philosophy is the page that quietly edits every other page.

Reading practice

Philosophy here functions as training in human questions: identity, meaning, choice, and what counts as a “good life.” This links outward to ventures (purpose), investing (uncertainty), and health (the body as constraint).

Film analysis

Abhay watches older films and analyzes them. In the logic of the artwork, each film is a “model” for a future: editing, pacing, character arcs. This is also why painting matters—see art practice.
